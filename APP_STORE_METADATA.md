## What is App Store Metadata?

App Store Metadata is the collection of text, images, and information that appears on your app's App Store page. It includes:
- App name and subtitle
- Description (what users see before downloading)
- Keywords (for search)
- Screenshots (visual preview)
- Privacy information
- Support URLs

This information helps users discover and understand your app before downloading it.
